Summary

Inception is a sci-fi thriller that delves into the world of dreams. Dom Cobb, a skilled thief, is given a chance to have his criminal history erased if he can successfully perform an inception: planting an idea into someone's subconscious. The movie explores complex themes of reality, memory, and the subconscious mind.
